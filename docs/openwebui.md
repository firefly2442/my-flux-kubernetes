# Open Web-UI

Open WebUI is an extensible, feature-rich, and user-friendly self-hosted AI
platform designed to operate entirely offline. It supports Ollama and
OpenAI-compatible APIs, making it a powerful, provider-agnostic solution
for both local and cloud-based models.

Open Web-UI is setup and connected to our Ollama service install.
